Frequently asked questions

How do I add my university's sports program to your platform?

We'd love to feature your university! Please contact our partnerships team through the form above or email partnerships@unisports.live with details about your program.

Can I get notifications for specific teams or games?

Absolutely! Once you create an account and log in, you can follow your favorite teams and set up custom notifications for games, scores, and news.

How accurate are your live updates?

We pride ourselves on accuracy. Our system combines automated data feeds with manual verification from our team of sports correspondents at each location.

Do you cover all university sports?

We currently cover 15 major sports across 200+ universities. We're constantly expanding our coverage based on user demand and available resources.
