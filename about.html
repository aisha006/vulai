About UniSports Live

Connecting university sports communities through real-time updates and interactive experiences

Our Mission

Revolutionizing university sports coverage

We're dedicated to bringing comprehensive, real-time coverage of university sports to students, alumni, and fans everywhere.

Real-time Updates

Get live scores, stats, and play-by-play updates from games across the country.

Interactive Map

Visualize game locations and track your favorite teams with our dynamic map.

Community Focused

Connect with fellow fans, alumni, and current students through our platform.

Our Journey

How we got here

  • Founded in 2018 by a group of university students who saw the need for better sports coverage.
  • First 10,000 users in 2019 as we expanded coverage to 25 universities.
  • Won Best Sports Tech at the 2020 University Innovation Awards.
  • Expanded nationwide in 2021, now covering over 200 universities and 15 sports.
  • 1 million users in 2023 with our most advanced features yet.
